MMAX Alerts

A data product to support account strategists' performance.

ABOUT THE PROJECT


MMAX Alerts is a daily email notification I built at Criteo to efficiently inform account strategists of their potential business opportunities.

It is difficult for account strategists to keep track of tens of clients while timely preparing for market seasonality (Christmas, Black Friday, etc.). The goal of this tool was to help them decide if they can pitch businesses to their clients by examining each account’s and its competitors’ KPIs.


All works are automated using R.


HOW IT WORKS


Some details have been omitted for confidentiality.

The underlying R code queries and checks 4 components:

  • YoY Comparison: did a client spend more with us one year ago?
  • Huge Influencer: Monitor spend by large accounts in a given market. If there is a more than 10 % increase in any one of them, flag the list of accounts with large cookie overlap.
  • Competitor Monitoring: For each account, identify top 5 competitors by measuring its performance relative to others. Query expected spend of each client and its competitors for the last 2 weeks. If their trend lines start diverging noticeably, flag it.
  • KPI Trend: Flag clients whose KPI started trending downward.
