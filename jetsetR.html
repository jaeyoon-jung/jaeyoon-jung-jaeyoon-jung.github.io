JetSetR

A twitter bot that helps you pick your next vacation spot.

ABOUT THE PROJECT


Twice a day, JetSetR picks a random city in the world and tweets 3 things to do and 1 place to stay, with photographs from Flickr. If you tweet at JetSetR with a specific city name, then the bot messages you the same content without the pictures.


All works are automated using R.


HOW IT WORKS


  1. Randomly pick a city from a pre-generated list of 100 top cities.
  2. Access a city-specific TripAdvisor link and scrape top 3 items from ‘Top-rate Things to Do’.
  3. From the ‘Places to Stay’ section, retrieve the names of 4 hotel recommendations.
  4. For each thing to do, generate a search link on Flickr, and download first image from it (most popular one).
  5. Generate a separate tweet for each thing to do- upload the downloaded Flickr image along with it.
  6. Pick one of the 4 hotels and tweet it with formatted text.
